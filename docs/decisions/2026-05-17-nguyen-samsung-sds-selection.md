## Findings

### Preliminary Observations

I expect improving profitability ratios because cloud and digital transformation services generally generate higher margins than traditional IT services.

I expect relatively stable liquidity ratios because Samsung SDS historically demonstrates strong cash generation and conservative financial management practices.

I expect segment profitability differences because logistics and IT service businesses operate under different cost structures and margin profiles.

### Ratio Categories Preview

The most relevant ratio categories will likely include profitability, liquidity, efficiency, and operating performance ratios.

Given the company’s industry structure, profitability and efficiency measures may provide insight into the effect of cloud growth and operating scale across business segments.
