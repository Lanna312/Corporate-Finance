## Findings

### Preliminary Observations

I expect improving profitability ratios from FY2024 to FY2025 because cloud and digital transformation services generally generate higher margins than traditional IT services.

I expect relatively stable liquidity ratios from FY2024 to FY2025 because Samsung SDS historically demonstrates strong cash generation and conservative financial management practices.

I expect segment profitability differences between logistics and IT services because these businesses operate under different cost structures and margin profiles.

### Ratio Categories Preview

The most relevant ratio categories will likely include profitability, liquidity, efficiency, and operating performance ratios.

Given the company’s industry structure, profitability and efficiency measures may provide insight into the effect of cloud growth and operating scale across business segments.
